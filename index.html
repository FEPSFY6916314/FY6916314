Operation Principles of RDEs


RDE operate on detonation combustion over the conventional deflagration combustion. The difference is are detonation reaction the reaction happen supersonically causing shock waves so the product travel in the same direction as the reaction while deflagration the rate of burning is subsonic and principle is like a candle the product travel in the opposite direction to the burn and occur layer by layer. (Venugopalan, 2015)
So in case of RDE these shockwave produced by combustion rotate in annular (ring shape) chamber with it blasting out the make but constantly being feed propellants to maintain the shock wave.
